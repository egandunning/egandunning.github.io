User personas

Collection of possible users.

  • Name: Haley
    Role: non-CS college student
    Age: 17-22
    Haley is a college student at PLU. She is studying something that is not related to computer science and does not know anything about encryption. She can use computer applications well, as long as the application is user friendly. She has many bank accounts and she has a different password for each bank. She hears about all these security breaches and wonders if her accounts are safe.

  • Name: Joe (Primary user)
    Role: CS college underclassman
    Age: 17-22
    Joe is a college student at PLU. He is studying computer science, but he has not taken any upper division CS courses and only knows vague details about encryption. He can use all but the most un-user friendly computer apps. Joe would like to know why he needs to be aware of encryption when writing programs.

  • Name: Matt
    Role: the critic
    Age: unknown
    Matt is a genius programmer. He knows how to do everything. He is quite picky on how he likes his applications set up and will criticize even the most minute details. He can work with un-user friendly applications, but he will complain constantly while he uses it. He already knows everything about encryption.
